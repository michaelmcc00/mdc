Sheperds Pie

Shepherd's pie is a classic comfort food dish made with a savory layer of ground meat (traditionally lamb) cooked with vegetables and gravy, topped with creamy mashed potatoes, then baked until golden brown. It's hearty, flavorful, and perfect for a cozy meal.

Ingrediants

  • Meat: Ground lamb or ground beef
  • Aromatics: Onions, garlic
  • Vegetables: Carrots, peas, and sometimes corn or celery
  • Liquid: Beef stock or broth
  • Thickener: All-purpose flour or cornstarch
  • Flavor enhancers: Worcestershire sauce, tomato paste, herbs (thyme, rosemary), salt, and pepper
